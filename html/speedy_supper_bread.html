Speedy Supper Bread 2.0

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Sirloin
  • Half a large red onion
  • 2 celery stalks
  • Handful of chopped spinach
  • 1 can stewed tomatoes -14.5 oz
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 6 oz of tomato paste
  • Any other veggies in the fridge that make sense can be used

Shred 5oz of parm on top of the filling in a 13 x 9 inch pyrex pan. Bake the filling for 15 minutes at 400 degrees to melt the cheese <- Very Important.

Topping

  • 15oz APF

Roll out topping and place on top of par cooked filling. Cross hatch top with knife/lame. Bake at 400 degrees for another 30 minutes or until the biscuits are done. Golden brown on top and not too soggy where they meet the cheese.
