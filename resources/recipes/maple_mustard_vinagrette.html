The Angry Trout's Maple Mustard Vinagrette

After our wedding we planned a camping and cabin trip to Grand Marais to celebrate and relax. The first two nights were cold and wet as we camped 30 feet from Superior's shore. We checked into our cabin on the third night. Looking for a good warm meal we made our way down to The Angry Trout. We're not sure why we ate anywhere else afterward! Whenever we want to escape to the North Shore we start streaming WTIP 90.7 and reach for this dressing we keep ready to go in a ball jar in the refridgerator.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup unrefined sunflower oil
  • ¼ cup dijon mustard (look for one with tumeric and no sugar)
  • ¼ cup real maple syrup
  • ¼ red wine vinegar

Method

  1. We like to whisk up the red wine vinegar, maple syrup and the dijon together in a bowl.
  2. Add this mixture to the food processor and then add the oil ¼ cup at a time. Giving the mixture a good blend between each ¼ cup of sunflower oil. If your food processor has the ability to mix while you pour the oil in slowly, do that.
