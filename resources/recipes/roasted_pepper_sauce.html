Roasted Red Pepper Sauce

Great as a pizza sauce. Use as pesto or add chunks of feta and eat as a dip. Drizzle over hummus.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan
  • ⅓ - ½ cup oil olive
  • 1 t oregano
  • 1 t basil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt and pepper
  • dash of red pepper flakes

Method

  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor. Add salt and pepper to taste.
