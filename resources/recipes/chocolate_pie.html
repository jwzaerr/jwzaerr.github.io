Easy Chocolate Pie

(based on Peg Stringer's recipe. Love you Grandama!)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham crackers (128g), crushed
  • 2 Tablespoons Powered Sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter (60g)
  • 10oz Chocolate*
  • 1/2 Pint Whipping Cream

    • *Traditional recipe is (6)Hershey bars. Alt. adaptation is (3)Hershey+(3)Dark Chocolate
