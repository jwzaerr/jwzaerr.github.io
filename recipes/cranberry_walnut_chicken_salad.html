Cranberry Walnut Chicken Salad

It's hearty, it's fresh, it's delicious!

Ingredients

Yields 4-6 servings

  • 1 3/4 of cooked chicken, chopped into bite size pieces
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 2 Celery ribs
  • 1 finally chopped shallot
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2-3/4 cup mayo
  • 3 T WHite wine vinegar
  • 2 T Fresh tarragon

Method

Toss all together and add salt and pepper to taste

Serve with fresh sourdough
