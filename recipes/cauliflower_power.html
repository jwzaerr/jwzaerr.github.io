Cauliflower Power

Inspired from a recipe by the New York Times.

Ingredients

Yields 4 servings

  • 1/4 cup almonds, roughly chopped and toasted
  • 2 medium heads of cauliflower
  • 3 T Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1/2 t crushed red pepper
  • 1 T minced garlic
  • 1 t Rosemary
  • 1/3 cup Chopped parsley
  • 1/2 t Lemon zest
  • 1 Roasted poblano
  • 1 Fresno chili, diced (sub for additional poblano or jalapeno)
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Method

Roughly chop almonds, toast over medium heat in skillet until aromatic. Set aside.

Cut cauliflower in quarters, remove core. Cut cauliflower quarter down to florets.

Heat oil in skillet over medium heat. Adjust if needed - keep it sizziling. Cook until tender when pierced with a fork, about 10-12 minutes.

Add roasted peppers, crushed red pepper, garlic, rosemary, parsley and lemon zest. Garnish with lemon wedge.
