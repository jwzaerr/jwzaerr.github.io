Kale Caesar Salad

A simple and quick salad that lasts well over night in the fridge to have as left overs for lunch the next day. We prefer the Curly Leaf Kale but Tuscan or others work just as well.

Ingredients

Yields 4-6 servings

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 t Dijon
  • 3/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 t Worcestershire
  • 1 Lemon
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

  • 14-16 oz Curly Leaf Kale

  • 3 oz. Parmesan grated
  • Panko bread crumbs, slightly toasted

Method

Add the first five ingredients and make the dressing. Use lemon to taste.

De-rib the kale and roughly chop and place in a salad bowl.

Squeeze 1/4 of a lemon over the kale. Massage gently to evenly coat the kale with the juice.

Add the dressing, parmesan and panko and toss to incorporate.

Serve and enjoy! Hail, Caesar? KALE CAESAR!
