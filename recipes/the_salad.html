The Salad

Adapted from Ann Stringer, this salad will have you shouting "CAN'T WE JUST EAT 'THE SALAD' AGAIN TONIGHT?!"

Ingredients

Yields 4 servings or 2 hungry people

  • Vinaigrette

  • 1 cup of Extra virgin olive oil
  • 2/3 cup Red wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, smash and mince it up.
  • 1 dash of cayenne
  • 1 heavy dash of Cholula Chipotle or hot sauce of choice.
  • 1/2 t oregano
  • 1/2 t dried mustard
  • 1 t salt

  • Mix all together and shake it up well.

  • Fixings

  • 1 small red onion, sliced thinly in half moons.
  • Mixed greens of choice
  • Pistachios, about 1/4 - 1/2 cup.
  • 1/4 cup Dried cranberries
  • 1 avicado, thin slices
  • Small block of feta

Method

Place mixed greens in a large bowl and mix in nuts and berries to your desired ratio. Add onion slices. Toss the salad with vinaigrette, then add in avocado and crumble feta on top.
