Banana Bread

A humble plot original! Whenever we have bananas that have a rough go in a bike bag or just get too ripe, we stick them in the freezer to use later for just this recipe. When you pull the bananas out to thaw it'd be a good time to pull that stick of butter out of the koolerator to soften too.

Ingredients

Yields one loaf

  • Wet ingredients

  • (4oz.) One stick of butter, softened
