Southwestern Potato Salad

Ingredients

Yields 6-8 servings

  • 3-4 lbs red potatoes
  • 4-6 strips of bacon
  • 1 poblano pepper
  • 3-4 green onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 T adobo sauce (Marie's mix: chili powder, onion powder, oregano, cumin and garlic)
  • 1 t Pepper
  • 1 t Lemon juice
  • 2 T Parsley
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Method

Heat oven to 375 degrees.

Rinse and clean potatoes, then chop and place on rimmed baking sheet. Bake for 40-50 minutes, until easily pierce with knife.

Grill poblano pepper over medium heat until skin is soft and charred. Place in bowl and cover in plastic wrap for 10 minutes. Then remove skin and seeds. Dice and set aside.

Cook bacon until crispy, set aside.

slice green onions, set aside.

Grate cheese, set aside.

Make dressing: combine 1/2 cup mayo, 1/2 cup sour cream in large mixing bowl. Add adobo seasoning, lemon, parsley and salt and pepper to taste.

Add the potatoes to the large mixing bowl with the dressing. Add bacon, poblano and cheese and mix to combine.

Chill in the fridge for 1 hour....if you can wait.
